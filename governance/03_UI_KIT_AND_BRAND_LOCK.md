# UI Kit and Brand Lock

## Authority

`shared/ui-kit` is the only owner of reusable visual tokens, themes, primitives, components, and layout archetypes.

## Forbidden Outside shared/ui-kit

- direct Tamagui import
- ui-kit deep imports
- local design systems
- raw hex colors
- screen-local reusable Button/Card/Header systems
- fontFamily definitions
- random spacing/radius/shadow systems

## Brand Colors

- Primary: #0A2F5C
- Accent: #FF500D
- Surface: #FFFFFF
