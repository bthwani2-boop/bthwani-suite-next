# Architecture Decision Alignment

Details the architectural alignment of the central catalog PIM & DAM structure.

- Categories are represented as hierarchical domains (L1) and nodes (L2-L4).
- Master Products (L5) reside at the core of the PIM.
- Store Assortments manage store-specific price and availability overrides.
- DAM assets are central, and links connect them to entities with approved/rejected states.
